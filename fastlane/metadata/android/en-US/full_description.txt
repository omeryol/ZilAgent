ZilAgent is a modern and user-friendly Android application designed to track school bell times and manage schedules effortlessly.

Key Features:
- Four different home screen widget styles (Modern, Panoramic, Circle and Horizontal).
- Full control over widget customization: colors, text size, and transparency.
- Customizable Exam Mode with large screen countdown support.
- Over 200 built-in inspiring quotes and the ability to add your own.
- 100% offline architecture with no internet permission required, ensuring complete privacy.
- Manage holidays and lesson schedules with ease.

ZilAgent is your stylish, open-source assistant for better time management in educational environments.
